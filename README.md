📊 E-Com Express Dashboard – Power BI Project
📌 Project Overview

The E-Com Express Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze and visualize key performance metrics of an e-commerce business. This dashboard provides valuable insights into revenue trends, order performance, product sales, and cancellation impact to support data-driven decision-making.

The goal of this project is to track business performance, identify revenue loss due to cancellations, and understand sales distribution across products, categories, states, and time periods.

🎯 Key Business Metrics

The dashboard includes the following KPIs:

💰 Total Revenue – Overall revenue generated from all completed orders

🛒 Average Order Value (AOV) – Average revenue per order

❌ Cancellation Rate (%) – Percentage of cancelled orders

📉 Lost Revenue due to Cancellations – Revenue impact from cancelled orders

📦 Total Sales – Total quantity of products sold

🔢 Order Count – Total number of Order IDs

📊 Data Visualizations

The dashboard includes the following analytical views:

📦 Revenue by Product Name – Identifies top-performing products

🏷️ Revenue by Category – Analyzes category-wise performance

🗺️ Revenue by State – Geographic revenue distribution

📅 Revenue by Year, Quarter & Month – Time-based sales trend analysis

🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

Power BI Desktop – Data modeling & dashboard creation

Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – KPI calculations and measures

Data Source – Structured e-commerce sales dataset

📈 Business Insights

This dashboard helps to:

Monitor overall business performance

Identify revenue leakage due to cancellations

Track product and category performance

Analyze seasonal trends and growth patterns

Support strategic decision-making with real-time KPIs

🚀 Project Outcome

The E-Com Express Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of e-commerce performance, enabling stakeholders to quickly understand sales patterns, revenue impact, and operational efficiency.
